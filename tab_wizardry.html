Knowledge Sacrifice:

The Knowledge Sacrifice is used to turn normal Bookshelfs into Knowledge Bookshelfs. The Sacrifice needs a entity to be killed above the block to work.

If a sound is played it means that it is working, but it is not every time, there is a chance of 1 in 10 (10%) to work. Any entity can start the sacrifice.

Knowledge Bookshelf:

Can be obtained when trying to sacrifice any entity above an Bookshelf. After Obtained it drops 3 Knowledge Books.

Knowledge Book:

Can be otained when breaking an Knowledge Bookshelf. It is used to change the Spawner Entity.

When opening the book it is required to write the mod id and the entity id with ":" in the middle. But it is required 64 Souls in the off-hand to sell and sign the book, to finnaly change to Spawner Entity.

Getting Souls:

There are two ways to get souls:

- Using Bottles:

When using a bottle in Soul Sand or Soul Soil [RMB], it is possible to collect souls to the bottle, then it can be possible to convert to souls

- Using Spawner Shards Sword:

It is possible to craft a "Spawner Shards Sword". When hitting entities with this weapon it has a possbility to extract souls from the entity.

Getting a Spawner:

Spawners can be obtained when breaking a Spawner with Silk Touch or with Spawner Shards and souls.

Spawner Shards are obtained breaking a Spawner without Silk Touch.

Spawner Shards:

Spawner Shards can be obtained by destroying a Spawner without the use of Silk Touch. Its used to create a Spawner Shards Sword to get Souls and to make Spawner Upgrades.

Spawner Core:

Spawner Core is used to upgrade Spawners when combined with minerals on the Smithing Table.
Can also be used to craft in the Spawner.

Spawner Upgrades:

Amethyst - Short: Decreases the ammount of mob spawnings

Emerald - Long: Increases the ammount of mob spawnings

Iron - Proximity: The player is required to be at least 8 blocks near the Spawner to work

Gold - Distance: The player is required to be at least 64

blocks near the Spawner to work

Redstone - Faster Core: Make Spawners faster

Diamond - Core Expansion: Increases the limit of entities that can be nearby to 24

Netherite - Core Evolution: Increases the limit of entities that can be nearby to 64
